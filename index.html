Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process for renting a house?

To rent a house, you typically need to find a suitable property, submit an application, provide necessary documents (like proof of income), pass a background and credit check, sign a lease agreement, and pay the first month's rent along with a security deposit.

What documents are required to rent a house?

Common documents include a valid ID, proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns), employment verification, rental history, and sometimes a reference letter from a previous landlord.

How long is the lease term?

Lease terms vary but are commonly 12 months. Some landlords may offer shorter terms (e.g., 6 months) or longer leases, depending on the property.
